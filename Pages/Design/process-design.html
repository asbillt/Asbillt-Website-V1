Process Simulations:

Process simulations are useful for determining and visualizing process data (e.g. pressures, temperatures, velocities, etc.) and are completed prior to the creation of 3D models. The goal for each simulation is to determine the optimal conditions for a process. The simulation output is extremely beneficial for determining the location of instrumentation. Also, process simulations are useful for optimizing existing facilities.

Benefits:

  • Reduce capital costs
  • Determine economical plant design
  • Optimize existing facilities
  • Increase response times
