CAD Drawings:

Detail drawings, also referred to as "fabrication drawings", are vital to the success of product development. They supply all the information to the manufacturing facility in order to turn raw material into finished parts and to correctly assembly any purchased equipment into the product. Detail drawings typically show an overview of what the completed product looks like and dives into the details of how to manufacture it along with a Bill of Materials (BOM) listing out all parts. Details can include how to machine and assemble parts, testing requirements, additional notes for the facility and numerous additional specification information.

Benefits:

  • Bill of Materials
  • Assembly details
  • Machining details
  • Testing details
  • Final product visualization
