Converting 2D to 3D:

The importance of converting drawings into models cannot be overstated. Many times organizations will have numerous legacy drawings without any 3D models. No 3D models means no updates can be made until that model is created. Creating a model from scratch when you have a drawing is a waste of company resources. It is significantly more efficient to convert a completed drawing into a model that is then usable to make any design changes.

Benefits:

  • Utilization of legacy drawings
  • Efficiency
  • Cost Savings
